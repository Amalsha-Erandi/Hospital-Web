About Care Compass Hospital

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion and expertise, ensuring the well-being of our community through innovative medical solutions and patient-centered care.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare institution, recognized for excellence in medical care, research, and community service, while setting new standards in patient care and medical innovation.

Our Journey

1998

Establishment of Care Compass Hospital with a focus on primary care services.

2005

Expansion of facilities to include specialized departments and advanced diagnostic center.

2015

Implementation of state-of-the-art technology and launch of digital health services.

2023

Achievement of international healthcare accreditation and opening of new research wing.
